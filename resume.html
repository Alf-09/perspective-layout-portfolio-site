Professional Narrative

Core Identity

After two decades of shaping physical visual identities and managing brand ecosystems for national corporate clients, I have transitioned into digital product design. My approach combines the discipline of a senior graphic designer with the systemic thinking of a front end developer. I don't just design interfaces, I construct scalable design systems with brand integrity and production efficiency.

I thrive at the intersection of aesthetics and code logic. By viewing digital design through a "production first" lens, I optimize developer handoffs, minimize technical debt, and ensure complex visual concepts translate flawlessly into user centered digital solutions.

Education & Evolution

My foundation in visual architecture was built at RMIT and hardened through nearly 20 years of managing highnvolume production pipelines, strict corporate client guidelines, and design operations at GPI Corporate and Bluegum. This intensive background instilled a rare discipline, I understand how to organize, package, and document creative assets so they are perfectly prepared for "manufacturing" whether that means physical printing or digital compilation.

To bridge physical design with digital product engineering, I have formalized my technical path through the Google UX Design Professional Certificate, Microsoft's Front-End Developer Program, and FreeCodeCamp's Full-Stack curriculum. This allows me to seamlessly collaborate with both creative directors and engineering teams.
